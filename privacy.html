Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

Summary: NetInfo does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your browsing activity never leaves your device.

What NetInfo does

NetInfo is a Safari browser extension for macOS that displays technical networking information about the website currently open in your browser tab, including IP address, TLS version, certificate details, HTTP protocol, and connection timing metrics.

Data we collect

NetInfo collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your browsing history
  • We do not store any information about the websites you visit
  • We do not collect your IP address
  • We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles
  • We do not transmit any data to our servers (we have none)

Third-party services

NetInfo uses the ipapi.co API to look up ASN and ISP information for a website's IP address. When you open the NetInfo popup on a website, the IP address of that website's server is sent to ipapi.co to retrieve provider information.

Please refer to ipapi.co's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle API requests.

NetInfo does not use any analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services.

On-device processing

All connection metrics — including TLS version, certificate expiry, HSTS status, HTTP protocol, and timing data — are gathered entirely on your device using Apple's built-in networking APIs. This data is displayed in the popup and immediately discarded when the popup is closed. Nothing is written to disk or sent anywhere.

Children's privacy

NetInfo does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. NetInfo will never introduce data collection without updating this policy first.
